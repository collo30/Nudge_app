About Me The curious explorer from Kenya

I'm Collins Cheruiyot, a Computer Science student from Kenya with an insatiable curiosity for how things work — from the physics of flight to the architecture of code.

My world revolves around exploring: whether it's attempting impossible landings in X-Plane (yes, I've landed a 737 on Courchevel's legendary short runway!), discovering niche music from Olivia Dean to ancient choral pieces like Miserere Mei Deus, or diving deep into Python and C++ projects.

I believe in fair treatment and love for all people — a value rooted in my Christian faith. I'm anti-trouble, always seeking to understand rather than judge, and fascinated by people and societies across the globe.
